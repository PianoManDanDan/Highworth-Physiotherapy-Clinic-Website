HOW TO FIND US

From Swindon

Follow the A361 Swindon Road towards Highworth. As you approach Highworth go past the shell garage in the right hand side. At the mini roundabout, continue straight over and turn right at the traffic lights onto the High Street. Highworth Physiotherapy Clinic can be found on the right hand side of the road just before you get to the market square.


From Lechlade

Follow the A361 towards Highworth. At the big roundabout at the bottom of the hill, go straight over and proceed up the hill on Lechlade Road. At the traffic lights at the top of the hill, turn left into the High Street. Highworth Physiotherapy Clinic can be found on the right hand side of the road just before you get to the market square.


From Blunsdon

Follow the B4019 and proceed along Cricklade Road approaching Highworth. At the traffic lights go straight over (small dog leg) into the High Street. Highworth Physiotherapy Clinic can be found on the right hand side of the road just before you get to the market square.


From Faringdon

Follow the B4019 and proceed up the hill on Eastrop road towards Highworth. Go straight over the double mini roundabout and proceed along the High Street. Highworth Physiotherapy Clinic can be found on the left hand side of the road just after you pass the market square.


Parking

There is free on street parking outside the clinic (1 hour parking limit); in the market square, 10 metres from the clinic (2 hour parking limit) or in the 2 car parks either side of Brewery Street, 50 metres from clinic (no parking restrictions).
