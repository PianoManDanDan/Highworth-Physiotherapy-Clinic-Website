LINKS

The links below are professional bodies and other websites which are referred to on other pages of this website. Please look at these websites to understand more about the role of physiotherapists and what they can do for you.



Facebook
www.facebook.com/HighworthPhysio

Visit our Facebook page, where we regularly post pictures and videos related to both human and animal physiotherapy.
CSP   The Chartered Society of Physiotherapy
www.csp.org.uk
Professional educational and trade union body for the UK's Chartered Physiotherapists.
HCPC   Health & Care Professions Council
www.hcpc-uk.org
The Health & Care Professions Council (previously HPC, The Health Professions Council until July 2012) regulate a number of health professions and police the quality of treatments the professions give.
Your physiotherapist must be a member of the HCPC to practice physiotherapy in the UK. You can check the register on their website to confirm that yours is registered.
Physio First   The Organisation of Chartered Physiotherapists in Private Practice
www.physiofirst.org.uk
The Physio First website has some very good advice on which medical conditions can benefit from physiotherapy.
AACP   Acupuncture Association of Chartered Physiotherapists
www.aacp.org.uk
Some physiotherapists follow post graduate courses to use acupuncture as a treatment. They are regulated by the AACP.
APPI   The Australian Physiotherapy and Pilates Institute
www.appihealthgroup.com
INFO
ACPAT   Association of Chartered Physiotherapists in Animal Therapy
www.acpat.org
Some of our physiotherapists are also trained to treat animals. ACPAT is the regulatory body for animal physiotherapy.
CKS   Clinical Knowledge Summaries
cks.nice.org.uk
This website is an official NICE (National Institute for Health and Care Excellence) wesite and contains a vast amount of independant information on all medical conditions. It also contains downloadable patient information leaflets to give you more information about your condition.
RAMP   Register of Animal Musculoskeletal Practitioners
www.rampregister.org
RAMP is a register of animal osteopaths, chiropractors and physiotherapists who all meet a gold standard of training. They are all fully insured and meet rigorous CPD standards on an annual basis.
